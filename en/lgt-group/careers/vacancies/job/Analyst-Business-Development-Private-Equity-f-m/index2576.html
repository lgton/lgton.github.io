Analyst - Business Development (Private Equity, f/m)

Country: CHE - Pfäffikon
Category: Asset Management

Job Information

LGT Capital Partners is a leading alternative investment specialist with USD 50 billion in assets under management and more than 400 institutional clients in 33 countries. An international team of over 300 professionals is responsible for managing a wide range of investment programs focusing on private markets, liquid alternatives, specialized long-only and multi-asset class solutions. Headquartered in Pfaeffikon (SZ), Switzerland, the firm has offices in New York, Dublin, London, Vaduz, Dubai, Beijing, Hong Kong, Tokyo and Sydney.

The successful candidate will be a part of the business development department based in Pfaeffikon, Switzerland. In this role, the successful candidate will focus especially on preparing presentations and other marketing documentation on our private equity offerings, and will also support relationship managers in preparing presentations for client discussions.

Your main responsibilities are:

  • Take ownership of maintaining and developing marketing materials, especially various private equity program presentations and due diligence questionnaires;
  • Create tailor-made client presentations;
  • Work closely together with the investment controlling, investment management, compliance and legal teams to ensure consistent communication and data output;
  • Provide client services support to relationship managers;
  • Respond to ad-hoc requests from clients, prospects and relationship managers.

Requirements

  • University degree in business administration, economics or similar;
  • University graduate or up to two years of prior work experience, preferably in the financial industry;
  • Sound understanding of capital markets and alternative investments;
  • High orientation in client services;
  • Native English, fluent German and additional language skills considered as a plus;
  • Excellent command of Microsoft Office, especially PowerPoint and Excel;
  • High attention to detail and accuracy;
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills;
  • Ability to work under pressure;
  • Team player.
